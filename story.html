PATCH NOTES - BEAT THE MACHINE

DAMON - THE COCKY CADET

The main protagonist of Beat the Machine. His cocky attitude and the I can do it all attitude might be the help that Sector 8 needs in defending the nasty attacks from Sector IX. While he might still be a rookie cadet in the eyes of Syncopia, who has extremely high intentions for people serving under her, Damon has had some previous experience in fighting evil.

T.O.M - THE B.A.D GUY

The evil villain, that's attacking Sector VIII. He's sent his army of Speed, Shell, and other virus in order to take over what's level of Sector VIII. With a love/hate relationship toward Syncopia, why does his wish to steal all the remaining power she has over the Origin Sectors?

Vitality Skills:

Agility Attacks -
Healing reduced from 2(+15% Armour)(+3% Damage) to 2(+8% Armour)(+2% Damage)

Hull Mending -
Cooldown increased from 60 seconds to 80 seconds.
Healing reduced from 75% of maximum health to 45% of maximum health.
Healing Duration lowered from 8 seconds to 4 seconds.


PATCH ALPHA 0.1

After internal playtesting from several family and friends. One thing was clear, the amount of damage that was coming from hazards felt a little off. Firewalls felt ineffective, and fans instanuked a player upon the smallest mistake. Firewalls in certain areas felt undodgable and we lowered their retrigger time to give the player more room to react.

Hazards:

Fan Damage: Decreased from 40 to 30.
Firewall Damage: Increased from 10 to 18.
Firewall Retrigger Rate: Increased from 1.0 seconds to 1.4 seconds.
